Vigor's Revenue Model

Sustainable Growth Strategy

Brand Engagement

Brands invest in Vigor to reach health-conscious consumers, creating a win-win relationship that drives sales while enhancing customer loyalty through exclusive offers and rewards.

User Rewards

Users earn points for healthy actions, redeemable for discounts and products, fostering a motivated community that values their health while ensuring continuous engagement with the app and partnered brands.

Data Insights

Vigor leverages user activity data to provide brands with valuable insights, helping them tailor marketing strategies and improving customer experiences while maximizing return on investment through targeted campaigns.

User Engagement

Win-Win System
